World's Biggest University

Annamacharya Institute of Technology and Sciences (AITS), located in Kadapa, Andhra Pradesh, is a private engineering college established in 2010 under the aegis
of the Annamacharya Educational Trust. Affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Anantapur, and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE),
AITS Kadapa offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology. The institute is committed to providing quality education, particularly to students from
the rural Rayalaseema region
