World's Biggest University

Annamacharya Institute of Technology and Sciences (AITS), located in Kadapa, Andhra Pradesh, is a private engineering college established in 2010 under the aegis of the Annamacharya Educational Trust. Affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Anantapur, and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), AITS Kadapa offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology. The institute is committed to providing quality education, particularly to students from the rural Rayalaseema region

Courses We Offer

At Annamacharya Institute of Technology & Sciences (AITS), we are committed to delivering high-quality education across a diverse range of disciplines. Our institution offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering, Management, and Computer Applications. Core engineering branches include Computer Science, Electronics and Communication, Electrical and Electronics, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering, each designed to equip students with industry-relevant skills and strong technical foundations. In addition, our MBA and MCA programs are tailored to develop leadership qualities and advanced computing expertise, preparing students for dynamic roles in the global marketplace. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a focus on innovation, AITS provides a holistic learning environment that fosters academic excellence and career readiness.

Computer Science And Engineering

The Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) department at AITS offers a future-ready curriculum focused on programming, AI, data science, and cybersecurity. Students gain hands-on experience through advanced labs, coding challenges, and real-time projects. Our expert faculty and industry tie-ups ensure strong academic and career guidance. The department prepares students to become skilled professionals in the evolving tech world.

Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)

The ECE department at AITS focuses on core areas like analog and digital electronics, communication systems, and VLSI design. Students gain practical skills through modern labs and hands-on projects. The curriculum is aligned with industry trends in embedded systems, IoT, and wireless communication. Graduates are well-prepared for careers in electronics, telecom, and automation sectors.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)

The EEE department at AITS covers key areas such as power systems, electrical machines, control systems, and renewable energy. Students gain hands-on experience through advanced labs and real-time simulations. The program integrates modern technologies like smart grids and energy management. Graduates are equipped for careers in power, automation, and electrical industries.

Civil Engineering (CE)

The Civil Engineering department at AITS focuses on structural engineering, construction technology, transportation, and environmental engineering. Students get practical exposure through fieldwork, lab experiments, and software tools like AutoCAD and STAAD Pro. The program emphasizes sustainable design and real-world problem solving. Graduates are prepared for roles in infrastructure development, planning, and public works.

Our global campus

The campus of Annamacharya Institute of Technology & Sciences (AITS) is a blend of academic excellence and serene natural surroundings, spread across 45 acres at the foothills of the sacred Seven Hills. Designed to nurture both intellect and innovation, the campus offers state-of-the-art infrastructure including modern classrooms, fully equipped laboratories, and a rich library that supports in-depth learning. AITS houses over 980 high-end computers across air-conditioned labs with access to various operating systems, ensuring a robust technical environment. The campus provides high-speed Wi-Fi, separate hostels for boys and girls, a hygienic canteen, sports facilities, and a dedicated health care center to ensure student well-being. With reliable transport services and an environment that encourages research, extracurricular activities, and community engagement, AITS stands as a holistic hub for students aspiring to grow academically and personally.

About US

Annamacharya Institute of Technology & Sciences (AITS), established with a vision to provide quality technical education, is a premier institution committed to academic excellence, innovation, and holistic student development. Located in a serene and inspiring environment, AITS offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, and computer applications. Our mission is to nurture technically skilled, ethically strong, and socially responsible professionals who can meet the challenges of a rapidly evolving global landscape. With experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, cutting-edge laboratories, and a strong focus on research and industry collaboration, AITS empowers students to turn knowledge into action and dreams into careers.
